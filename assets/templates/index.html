Easy To Clean

Walk-in showers mostly have flat surfaces where there are fewer curves and areas that collect dirt and grime

Water Efficiency

A shower traditionally uses less water than bathtubs, posing an opportunity to use less water and energy from your water heater

Slip Resistant

Slips in the shower can pose an extreme risk, our shower pans feature slip resistant material to prevent accidents in the shower

Increased Space

A walk-in shower allows for a larger footprint in your bathroom while old tub enclosures waste space by limiting the square footage of your space

Can Fit Any Bathroom
